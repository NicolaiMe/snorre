Notifications are used to provide the user with information and feedback on actions.

<div style={{marginTop: '60px'}}></div>

### Default
Used for general information.
<Preview>
    <Story name="Default">
        <Alert>Here is some general information.</Alert>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Success alert
Used to give the user a positive feedback. For example: Your inquiry has been sent.
<Preview>
    <Story name="Success">
        <Alert success>Thank you for feedback!</Alert>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Info alert
Used for notice of maintenance or alteration.
<Preview>
    <Story name="Info">
        <Alert info>The service is down between 23:00 - 05:00.</Alert>
    </Story>
</Preview>

### Danger alert
Used where an error has occurred.
<Preview>
    <Story name="Danger">
        <Alert danger>There has been a mistake. Could not reach service.</Alert>
    </Story>
</Preview>
